Welcome to Happy's Page!

About My Cat

My cat's name is Happy. He is a black and white Maine Coon.

What I Love About Happy

Happy is very friendly and playful. Even though he is a big cat, he loves to sit on my lap. Every day when I get home, he rushes to greet me and also stretches his body.

Fun Facts

Happy loves to eat white steamed bread. His favorite toy is a plush toy fox that can make a sound. Maine Coons are known for their large size and fluffy tails, and Happy is no exception!

My Favorite Memory

One time, Happy chased his own tail for five minutes and then looked at me like nothing happened. It made me laugh so hard～
