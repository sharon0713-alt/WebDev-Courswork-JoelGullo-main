Welcome to Sara's Kitchen

Sara lives in Brooklyn, NY, but her heart is in Trinidad and Tobago. Every summer, she visits her grandparents and learns traditional family recipes.

Sara's Favorite Dish: Doubles

Doubles is fried dough filled with curry chana (chickpeas). It is topped with tamarind sauce and is absolutely delicious!

Sara's Favorite Colors

Sara loves red and gold. Red reminds her of the Trinidad and Tobago flag. Gold reminds her of the warm sun.

Why This Content?

I chose these items because they reflect Sara's love for family, culture, and cooking. The red and gold colors represent her heritage. The dish doubles is a symbol of her connection to her grandmother and Trinidad.
